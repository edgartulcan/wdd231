ABOUT Tuki

¡Welcome!

This agency was born in 2020 with a simple mission: to help others to find a good home. A good place to live. Starting with just a few listings in Guayaquil, but now we have apartments in different locations in all the city.

Today, we are proud to be one of the most trusted rental platforms in the region. With apartments available throughout the city, we offer rentals without the need for bank deposits prior to viewing. We also specialize in providing guidance to new tenants so they can access all the benefits. If you require more information, please click on "Contact Us."

Our mission

Our mission is to offer reliable, accessible, and modern housing solutions, building a simple and personal experience every step of the way. We want everyone looking for an apartment to feel at ease, supported, and confident that they are making the best decision for their well-being.

In a vibrant city like Guayaquil, where life moves fast and opportunities grow every day, we work with passion to connect our clients with spaces that allow them to live better. We believe in transparency, prompt service, and treating each client with the respect and warmth of someone who is part of our community.
